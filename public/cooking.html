Grilled Salmon with Asparagus
STEP 1 of 5 20% COMPLETE
06:30

Season the Salmon

Rinse the salmon fillets under cold water and pat them completely dry with paper towels. Season both sides generously with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper. Drizzle a small amount of olive oil over each fillet.

Chef's Tip: Let the salmon sit at room temperature for 15 minutes before cooking for even results.
04:30

Prepare the Salmon Fillets

Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. This ensures a crispy skin when searing. Season generously with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of fresh lemon juice on both sides.

Chef's Tip: Use room temperature salmon for even cooking.
07:00

Sear the Salmon

Heat the non-stick pan over medium-high heat. Place the salmon fillets skin-side down. Cook for 4 minutes without moving. Flip gently and cook for another 3 minutes.

Chef's Tip: Don't move the fish while searing — let the crust form naturally.
05:00

Prepare the Asparagus

Trim the woody ends of the asparagus. Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill or roast alongside the salmon for about 5 minutes until tender-crisp.

Plate and Serve

Arrange the asparagus on a plate. Place the salmon fillet on top. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over everything and garnish with fresh dill if desired. Serve immediately.
